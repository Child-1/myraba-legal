Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Myraba App — Early Access

Myraba ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Myraba mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Full name, phone number, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash).
  • Identity information: BVN or NIN submitted for KYC verification (masked and encrypted at rest).
  • Transaction data: Records of transfers, bill payments, thrift contributions, and gift transactions.
  • Device information: Basic device identifiers for security and fraud prevention.
  • Usage data: In-app activity to improve features and detect fraud.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your Myraba account and wallet.
  • To process transactions, thrift contributions, bill payments, and gift sends.
  • To verify your identity (KYC) as required for financial services.
  • To send OTP codes and security alerts via email or SMS.
  • To comply with applicable Nigerian financial regulations.
  • To improve and personalise your in-app experience.
  • To prevent fraud and unauthorised access.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Payment processors: Flutterwave — to process external card transactions.
  • Bill payment providers: VTpass — to fulfil airtime, data, and utility payments.
  • KYC providers: Dojah — to verify BVN/NIN identity information.
  • Email providers: Resend — to deliver OTP and transactional emails.
  • Regulatory bodies: When required by Nigerian law or a valid court order.

All third-party providers are bound by their own privacy policies and applicable data protection laws.

4. Data Security

We take security seriously. Sensitive fields (BVN, NIN) are encrypted using AES-256 at rest. All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS. Passwords are hashed using BCrypt and never stored in plain text. JWT tokens expire after 24 hours.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active or as required by Nigerian financial regulations (typically 5–7 years for transaction records). You may request account deletion by contacting us — see Section 8.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data (subject to regulatory retention requirements).
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential data processing.

7. Children's Privacy

Myraba is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately.

For privacy concerns, data requests, or account deletion:

Email: hello@myraba.com

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. We will notify users of significant changes via in-app notification. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
